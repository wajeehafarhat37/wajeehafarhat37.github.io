                                                            What is web
                                                                hosting and do I need it?
                                                        


                                                        

                                                            
Web hosting is a service that allows individuals and
                                                                organizations to make their websites accessible on the
                                                                internet.

                                                        

                                                    

                                                    

                                                        

                                                            What can I
                                                                use to build my website?
                                                        


                                                        

                                                            
You can use various tools and platforms to build your
                                                                website, depending on your technical proficiency and
                                                                specific needs.

                                                        

                                                    

                                                

                                            

                                        

                                    

                                

                                

                                    

                                        

                                            

                                                

                                                    

                                                        

                                                            What type of
                                                                web hosting do I need?
                                                        


                                                        

                                                            
The type of web hosting you need depends on the size and
                                                                complexity of your website. For smaller sites or
                                                                personal blogs, basic hosting is often sufficient and
                                                                cost-effective.
